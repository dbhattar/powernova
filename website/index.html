About PowerNOVA

PowerNOVA is the next-generation intelligence platform for the power generation industry. We aggregate, analyze, and present critical data from interconnection queues across all major ISO/RTO markets in North America.

Our platform combines advanced search capabilities, real-time data processing, and intuitive user interfaces to provide unprecedented visibility into the evolving energy landscape. From renewable energy developers to grid operators, PowerNOVA empowers stakeholders to make informed decisions in an increasingly complex market.
